                                                           Quantization
                   (This annex forms an integral part of this Recommendation | International Standard.)


In this annex, the flow charts and tables are normative only in the sense that they are defining an output that alternative
implementations shall duplicate.
This annex specifies the forms of inverse quantization used for the reconstruction of tile-component transform
coefficients. Information about the quantization of transform coefficients for encoding is also provided. Quantization is
the process by which the transform coefficients are reduced in precision.

E.1.2     Reversible transformation

E.1.2.1   Determination of the quantization step size
For reversible transformation, the quantization step size ╬öb is equal to one (no quantization is performed).
